## Tips for Best Results

1. **Model**: Use FLUX Schnell or FLUX Dev on OpenArt for fastest results
2. **Resolution**: Set to 1200x800 or 1200x675 (landscape)
3. **Negative prompt** (if supported): "text, watermark, logo, blurry, low quality, cartoon, illustration, people, human"
4. **Steps**: 20-30 for best quality
5. **CFG**: 3.5-7.0
6. **Generate 2-3 variations** of each and pick the best one
7. **Save as**: `images/[service-name]-hero.jpg` (e.g., `images/roofing-hero.jpg`)

After generating, place all images in `tools/website/nwgeneralcontractor/images/` directory.
