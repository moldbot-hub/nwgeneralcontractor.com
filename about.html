About NW General Contractor

A local team that takes pride in building things right. Serving Everett and Snohomish County with quality craftsmanship and honest pricing.

David — Owner & General Contractor

Built on Quality, Trust, and Hard Work

NW General Contractor was founded with a simple idea: homeowners in Snohomish County deserve a contractor they can actually trust. Someone who shows up when they say they will, communicates honestly about costs and timelines, and delivers work they are genuinely proud of.

We are a full-service general contracting company based in Everett, Washington. We handle everything from kitchen and bathroom remodeling to roofing, siding, foundation repair, and complete home renovations. Whether you need a single room updated or your entire home transformed, we manage the project from start to finish so you do not have to.

Our Story

NW General Contractor started from a real passion for building and a frustration with how the industry often treats homeowners. Too many people in the Everett area have been burned by contractors who overpromise and underdeliver — missed deadlines, surprise costs, poor communication, or work that does not hold up.

We set out to do things differently. Every project begins with a detailed, written estimate. We keep you informed throughout the process, return your calls and messages promptly, and stand behind our work long after the last nail is driven. Our reputation is built one project at a time, and we take that seriously.

As a small, hands-on team, we bring the kind of personal attention that larger companies cannot match. You will not be passed between sales reps and project managers who have never picked up a hammer. The people you meet during your estimate are the same people who will be building your project.

What We Do

We are a full-service general contractor, which means we handle a wide range of residential construction and remodeling projects:

  • Interior Remodeling — Kitchens, bathrooms, flooring, painting, and whole-home renovations. We handle the design coordination, demolition, rough-in, and finish work.
  • Exterior Work — Roofing, siding, windows, doors, and foundation repair. We protect your home from the Pacific Northwest weather with quality materials and expert installation.
  • Outdoor Living — Decks, patios, pergolas, fencing, outdoor kitchens, and concrete work. We help you make the most of your property year-round.
  • Additions & New Construction — Home additions, ADU construction, and detached garages. We manage the full build from permitting through final inspection.
  • Custom Carpentry — Built-in shelving, custom trim, wainscoting, mantels, and specialty woodwork that adds character and function to your home.

Our Approach

  • Transparent Pricing — Detailed written estimates with itemized costs. No hidden fees, no surprise change orders. If something changes mid-project, we discuss it with you before any additional work happens.
  • Quality Materials — We source materials from trusted suppliers and choose products rated for Pacific Northwest conditions. From moisture-resistant framing to UV-stable exterior finishes, every selection is made with longevity in mind.
  • Clear Communication — You will have a dedicated point of contact throughout your project. Regular updates, responsive to calls and texts, and honest about timelines. If something comes up, you will hear about it from us before you have to ask.
  • Licensed & Protected — Fully licensed with Washington State, carrying general liability insurance and a surety bond. Your home and your investment are protected.
  • Clean Job Sites — We respect your home. Daily cleanup, protective coverings on floors and fixtures, and careful attention to your landscaping and property throughout the project.

Serving Snohomish County

We are proud to serve homeowners across Snohomish County, including Everett, Marysville, Lake Stevens, Lynnwood, Mukilteo, Snohomish, Mill Creek, Bothell, and Arlington. As a local company, we understand the unique character of each community — from the historic craftsman homes of downtown Everett to the newer developments in Lake Stevens and the waterfront properties along Mukilteo.

Snohomish County is one of the fastest-growing regions in Washington state, and much of the existing housing stock is 30 to 50 years old or more. Whether you are updating an aging kitchen, replacing a worn-out roof, converting a garage into an ADU, or completely renovating a mid-century home, we have the experience to handle the surprises that come with opening up walls and working on established homes.
