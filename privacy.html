Privacy Policy

How NW General Contractor LLC collects, uses, and protects your information.

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

NW General Contractor LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry or request an estimate through our website, phone, or in person, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Email address
  • Project details, preferred service, and city
  • Any additional information you choose to share about your project

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide estimates
  • Schedule consultations and on-site visits
  • Send project updates, appointment confirmations, and follow-up communications
  • Fulfill contracts and deliver services you’ve requested

3. SMS / Text Messaging

By opting in on our contact form, you consent to receive SMS text messages from NW General Contractor LLC at the phone number you provided, regarding your project inquiry, appointment confirmations, estimates, and follow-up communications.

  • Message frequency varies based on your project stage and communication needs.
  • Message and data rates may apply per your mobile carrier’s plan.
  • Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from SMS messages. You will receive a confirmation message and no further SMS communications.
  • Reply HELP at any time for support, or contact us at (425) 286-5639.
  • Opting out of SMS will not affect our ability to contact you about your project by phone or email.

No Sharing of Mobile Information: Your mobile information (phone number and SMS consent) will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties for promotional or marketing purposes. Mobile opt-in data and consent are not shared with any third party, and we do not share this information with affiliates for their marketing purposes.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only with:

  • Trusted service providers who help us operate our business (e.g., SMS delivery, email, scheduling) and who are contractually required to protect your information
  • Subcontractors directly working on your project, only as needed to perform the work
  • Government or legal authorities when required by law

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at the details below. You may also opt out of any communications at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective Date” above reflects the most recent update.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, contact us at:
