## STEP 2: File LLC ($200, 10 minutes)

**Go to:** https://www.sos.wa.gov/corps/llc_formOnline.aspx

1. Click "File Online"
2. LLC Name: **NW General Contractor LLC**
3. Registered Agent: Use yourself (David) at your home address, OR use a registered agent service (~$50/yr)
4. Principal Office: Your home address (this IS public record)
5. Management: Member-managed (you manage it yourself)
6. Pay $200 filing fee
7. Save your confirmation — you'll get your UBI number

**Expedited processing:** Add $50 for same-day or next-day processing

## STEP 3: Get EIN (FREE, 5 minutes)

**Go to:** https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online

1. Click "Apply Online Now"
2. Select "Limited Liability Company (LLC)"
3. Select "Started a new business"
4. Enter your LLC name and address
5. You'll receive your EIN INSTANTLY on screen
6. Print/save the confirmation letter (SS-4)

---

## STEP 4: WA State Business License ($15)

**Go to:** https://dor.wa.gov/open-business/apply-business-license

1. Click "Apply for a Business License"
2. Enter your UBI number (from LLC filing)
3. Enter your EIN
4. Select business type: "Construction — General Contractor"
5. Pay $15
6. Processing: ~10 business days (you can operate while pending)

## STEP 7: L&I Contractor Registration ($141)

**After you have your bond and insurance:**

**In-person (FASTER):**
- Everett L&I Office: 730 134th St SW, Suite 200, Everett, WA 98204
- Bring: Bond proof, insurance certificate, notarized Form F625-001-000, $141 payment

**Online:**
- Go to: https://lni.wa.gov/licensing-permits/contractors/register-as-a-contractor/
- Download Form F625-001-000
- Get it notarized (UPS Store, bank, etc. — usually free at your bank)
- Submit with bond and insurance documents
- Processing: 2-4 weeks

**IMPORTANT:** You CANNOT legally do work or advertise contractor services until this registration is active. You CAN set up your web presence, generate leads, give estimates, and sign contracts while waiting.
